Advertising and Affiliate Links

FinGuid ("we," "us," or "our") participates in various affiliate marketing programs, which means we may get paid commissions on purchases made through our links to retailer sites.

How This Supports Our Mission

These affiliate relationships help us maintain and grow FinGuid while keeping our calculators and resources free for all users. The small commissions we earn:

  • Help cover hosting and development costs

We only recommend products and services that we genuinely believe will provide value to our users.

Our Review Process

We maintain strict editorial independence. Our recommendations are based on:

  1. Extensive research of available products
  2. Analysis of features, pricing, and user reviews

  4. User feedback and experiences

The compensation we receive may influence which products we review and recommend, but it never affects our opinions or evaluations.

Transparency Promise

We believe in complete transparency with our users. Therefore:

  • We clearly disclose affiliate relationships
  • We never allow advertisers to influence our content
  • We provide honest reviews and comparisons
  • We prioritize user needs above potential commissions
