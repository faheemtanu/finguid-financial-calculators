Home Loan FAQ & Financial Literacy

What is PITI?

PITI stands for Principal, Interest, Property Tax, and Home Insurance. It represents the four core components of a standard monthly mortgage payment. Our AI insights help you manage all four components effectively.

How does an extra payment help me?

Every extra dollar you pay goes directly to the loan principal. This reduces the balance faster, meaning you pay less interest over the life of the loan and shorten your term. Our calculator shows the exact savings!

Is the FRED Rate live?

Yes, we pull the official 30-Year Fixed Mortgage Rate from the Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED) API. This ensures you have the most reliable baseline data for your American home-buying decision.

Why is the AI Insight feature important?

Our AI Insight Engine uses proprietary logic based on your inputs to offer personalized financial recommendations, such as when to eliminate PMI or how much interest you can save with a 15-year term, guiding you to a smarter loan decision.
