Understanding Refinancing

Refinancing your mortgage can save you money if the new interest rate is significantly lower than your current rate. However, you need to consider closing costs and how long you plan to stay in the home.

AI Tip

Generally, refinancing makes sense if you can reduce your interest rate by at least 0.75% to 1% and plan to stay in your home long enough to recoup the closing costs through monthly savings.
